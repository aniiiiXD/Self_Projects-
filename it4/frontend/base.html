Meet the Mentor

Hi 🙂 I am Md. Moin, lead mentor at BPrep. I did my summer internship at HUL and received a PPO from the company to join their leadership program. Currently, I am pursuing MBA from IIT Bombay- SJMSOM.

After clearing CAT with a 98.68 %ile, I received interview calls from reputed B Schools like- IITB, SP Jain, MDI, IITD, IIMs etc. I have appeared for interviews of multiple companies like- Kearney, Asian Paints, HUL, ITC etc. This experience taught me the intricacies of professional interviews.

I am passionate about sharing my knowledge and experience. I have trained 50+ students for their MBA interviews. At BPrep, I aim to provide personalized, one-on-one mentorship to help you navigate interviews and achieve your professional goals.
